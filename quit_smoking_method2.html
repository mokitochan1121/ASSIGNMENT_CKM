    • SMOKING CESSATION TIPS

    Step One

    As you are getting ready to quit, stop buying cigarettes.

    Step Two

    When your quit date arrives, throw out anything that reminds you of smoking.

    Step Three

    Prepare a list of things to do when a craving hits.

    Step Four

    Make an appointment with an acupuncturist.

    Final Step

    Put all the money you are saving on cigarettes in a large glass jar.
